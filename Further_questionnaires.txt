Further questionnaires:

DASS-21 (Depression Anxiety Stress Scales) - Measures depression, anxiety, and stress simultaneously
PANAS (Positive and Negative Affect Schedule) - Measures positive and negative emotional states
PSS (Perceived Stress Scale) - Measures perception of stress
WHO-5 (WHO Well-Being Index) - Brief measure of positive well-being
PCL-5 (PTSD Checklist) - For trauma-related symptoms
Social Support Questionnaire - Measures perceived social support
UCLA Loneliness Scale - Measures feelings of loneliness and social isolation
Brief Resilience Scale - Measures ability to bounce back from stress
